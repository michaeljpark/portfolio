A short introduction

Approach

I design fast, with intent.

My work centres on identifying meaningful design value through user-centred, collaborative processes. I prototype quickly with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to test usability, gather feedback, and refine — with a strong emphasis on interaction quality and accessibility.

At the macro level, I align stakeholders around clear user goals and the purpose behind each design decision.
