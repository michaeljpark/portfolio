Approach

I design fast, with intent.

My process runs on speed and intent. I adopt emerging tools first, using them as leverage to move fast and think clearly. Research and documentation are how I communicate with stakeholders: data-driven artifacts that surface what matters, align perspectives, and keep the project pointed in the right direction.

At the core is design strategy: structuring qualitative and quantitative research, establishing clear KPIs, and translating findings into visualizations that make decisions easier and faster to reach.
